Spiffout - The Future of Consumer Engagement

A web based consumer engagement platform which connects consumers to businesses and provides consumer satisfaction analytics. We are trying to remove the the obstacle between the product owner and consumer in the present world. The problem arise when a product owner does not get the true analytics of their product in market and believe on surveys and analytics with in organisation but spiffout provide a direct communication of a product owner to customer with analytics of emotion, sentiments, opinion area and satisfication.

 Contribution

I designed and implemented Graphical User Interface for web based platform. The design was aim to buila centralized dashboard for the user to show analysis and analytic in an efficent way. I was also involed in designs of application and spifffout social plugin.
