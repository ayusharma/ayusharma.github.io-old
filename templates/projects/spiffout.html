Spiffout - The Future of Consumer Engagement

A web based consumer engagement platform which connects consumers to businesses and provides consumer satisfaction analytics. We are trying to remove the obstacle between the product owner and consumer in the present world. The problem arises when a product owner does not get the true analytics of their product in the market and believe on surveys and analytics within organizations, but spiffout provide a direct communication of a product owner to customer with analytics of emotion, sentiments, opinion area and satisfaction.

Contribution

I designed and implemented Graphical User Interface for web based platform. The design was aim to build centralized dashboard for the user to show analysis and analytic in an efficent way. I was also involved in designs of application and spifffout social plugin.
