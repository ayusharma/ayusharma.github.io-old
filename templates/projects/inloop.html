Inloop - Better Discussions

Problem Statement: Communication among the project members is very necessary for an effective and efficient product. Even Slack, Basecamp and Yammer solved the problem of communication with an email alternative but discovery of content was also a tough task. When a project is to be categorized in different groups like design and development and the new content is to be shared among team member in the discussion in real time and discussion leads to mesh.
Solution: We created in loop in InnovAccer Summer Entrepreneur Program to solve how teams share and discuss things they love. In Inloop, you can discover new content, push articles to personalize groups and discuss around entities.
