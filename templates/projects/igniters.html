Igniters - Open Learning Platform

First Year - First Semester:
Problem Statement: When I joined college, surprisingly found that there is a lot of text books, practical manuals and notebooks. It's too heavy to carry. I thought about a digital platform to reduce the requirements of books to carry. Even in holidays, I have to take some text books to prepare for exams. Solution: From the childhood I am open to learning. Learn new things and quickly implement them. I took this project to make introduction to the web and technologies. I started building the website by seeing MOOC.
I made an online platform to access general information about classes along with the class notes and practical manuals.
