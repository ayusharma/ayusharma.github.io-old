Dailyrounds - Clinical Cases

Problem Statement: Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is very important for growing business and web traffic. Good SEO increases the user traffics and trend webpage in Search Engine Results. Client side Javascript MVC frameworks have serious problems from an SEO point of view. Most of the search engine still do not properly crawl JS components. In Daily rounds, My task is to develop an SEO friendly MVC platform for Clinical Cases.
Solution: Rendering of HTML from server increase crawl quality for search engines. We are using Jinja2 templeting engine to serve content along with caching and HTML5 Local Storage APIs.
