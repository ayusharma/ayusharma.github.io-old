Dailyrounds - Clinical Cases

Problem Statement: Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) is very important for growing business and web traffic. Good SEO increase the user traffics and trend webpage in Search Engine Results. Client side Javascript MVC framewoks have serious problems from a SEO point of view. Most of the search engine still do not properly crawl JS components. In Dailyrounds, My task is to develop a SEO friendly MVC platform for Clinical Cases.
Solution: Renderring of HTML from server increase crawl quality for search engines. We are using Jinja2 templeting engine to serve content along with caching and HTML5 Local Storage APIs.
